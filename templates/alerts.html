How It Works

The alert system automatically sends health risk predictions via SMS to all registered users every hour. The system:

  1. Fetches weather data for each user's city
  2. Predicts health risk using the trained model
  3. Sends SMS alerts with disease risk, risk level, and precautions

Troubleshooting SMS Issues

If alerts are not being sent, check the following:

  • Twilio Trial Account: If you're using a Twilio trial account, you must verify recipient phone numbers first. Verify numbers here
  • Daily Limit: Trial accounts are limited to 50 messages per day. Upgrade your account to send more.
  • Phone Format: Ensure phone numbers are in E.164 format (e.g., +91XXXXXXXXXX)
  • Account Balance: Check that your Twilio account has sufficient balance
  • Credentials: Verify your Twilio Account SID and Auth Token are correct
